The Zomato Restaurant Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Python, MySQL, and Power BI. The project focuses on analyzing restaurant performance, customer preferences, pricing strategies, delivery trends, and operational KPIs to transform raw restaurant data into meaningful business insights.



🎯 Project Objectives-



* Analyze restaurant performance across multiple cities.
* Identify the most popular cuisines and restaurant categories.
* Compare revenue, profit, and order trends.
* Evaluate customer ratings and restaurant popularity.
* Analyze payment methods and delivery performance.
* Build an interactive dashboard to support business decision-making.



🛠️ Tools \& Technologies-



* Power BI
* MySQL
* Python (Pandas)
* DAX
* Power Query
* Microsoft Excel





📂 Dataset Information-



* Dataset: Zomato Restaurant Dataset
* Total Records: 210,908
* Cities Covered: 99
* Restaurant Names: 40,900
* Restaurant Locations: 3,730
* Cuisine Combinations: 9,360
* Restaurant Categories: 27
* Price Categories: 4
* Rating Categories: 5
* Popularity Categories: 5



📊 Dashboard KPIs-



Total Revenue

Total Profit

Total Orders

Average Rating

Average Delivery Time

Total Restaurants



💡 Key Business Insights-



* North Indian cuisine generated the highest number of orders.
* Around 75% of all orders were successfully delivered.
* Bangalore generated the highest overall revenue.
* Cash \& Card remained the most preferred payment method.
* Highly popular restaurants contributed the highest order volume.
* Mid-range restaurants recorded the largest share of customer orders.
